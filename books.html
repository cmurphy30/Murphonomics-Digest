Books

In This Economy?

Kyla Scanlon

A sharp, accessible breakdown of how the modern economy actually works — and why it so often feels broken. Scanlon cuts through jargon to explain everything from inflation to vibes-based recessions.

The Economic Consequences of Mr. Trump

Philip Coggan

A clear-eyed analysis of what Trump's trade war means for the global economy — drawing on history, data, and the lessons of past protectionist eras to assess the real costs of tariffs and economic nationalism.

Arguing with Zombies

Paul Krugman

Krugman takes on the economic myths that refuse to die — from trickle-down tax cuts to the perils of government debt — laying out what mainstream economics actually says and why it matters for policy.

The Neatest Little Guide to Stock Market Investing

Jason Kelly

A no-nonsense primer on how the stock market works — covering everything from reading a balance sheet to building a long-term portfolio. Accessible enough for beginners, practical enough to keep on your shelf.
